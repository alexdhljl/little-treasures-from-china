# Auctus Heritage Logo Guidelines

## Primary Logo

The primary Auctus Heritage logo is the horizontal composition:

```text
AH geometric symbol + AUCTUS HERITAGE wordmark
```

Use:

```text
frontend/public/brand/logo-horizontal.svg
```

## Secondary Logo

The secondary mark is the AH geometric symbol only.

Use:

```text
frontend/public/brand/logo-icon.svg
```

## Minimum Size

Digital desktop:

```text
Minimum width: 160px
Preferred header width: 280px to 320px
```

Digital mobile:

```text
Minimum width: 150px for horizontal logo
Use icon only below 120px available width
```

Print:

```text
Minimum horizontal logo width: 30mm
Minimum icon width: 12mm
```

## Clear Space

Keep clear space around the logo equal to at least the width of the smallest vertical bar in the AH symbol.

Do not place navigation labels, borders, images, or buttons inside this clear space.

## Color

Primary:

```text
Black on warm white or white backgrounds.
```

Reverse:

```text
White on black or dark backgrounds.
```

Use the warm white page background and black logo as the default website expression.

## Incorrect Usage

Do not:

- stretch the logo
- compress the logo
- rotate the logo
- recolor the logo arbitrarily
- add shadows
- add gradients
- add outlines
- change the AH mark spacing
- change stroke widths or rectangle proportions
- separate the wordmark from the AH symbol in the primary logo
- place the logo over busy photography without sufficient contrast

## Production Notes

The logo should remain sharp on Retina and high-resolution displays. Prefer SVG for all browser-rendered surfaces. Use PNG only where the platform requires a raster icon, such as Apple touch icons.
